FAQ.

Is it free? +

100% free. No purchase necessary for repairs, the quest, or the lucky draw. We mean it.

Do I need to bring my own item? +

Yes, bring one item you want to repair. We provide the thread, patches, and guidance.

What if my item can't be fixed? +

Our team will do their best. If it's truly unrepairable, we'll help you safely retire it and maybe repurpose the fabric into something new.
